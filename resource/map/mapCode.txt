Four-digit number: 
First represents type;
Second represents UpDown, 1 Up 2 Down 0 NoUpDown; 
Third represents LeftMidRight, 1 Left 2 Mid 3 Right 0 NoLeftRight; 
Fourth represent  the  sequence number.

timberpile or fence: 1
road: 2
lawn or box or tree or grass: 3
pool: 4
house: 5
wall or window or door: 6
stairs or lamp: 7
cliff: 8
